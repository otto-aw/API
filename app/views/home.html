Why You Should Look for New Car Rebates:

New Car Rebates are cash back offers or incentive offers that the manufacturer offers you, the consumer in order to entice you to buy their car. The reason for rebates are the ability to move more cars and trucks off the lots to make room for new ones, or if sales for those cars are not what was forecasted... What does that mean to you? You can get a car for a great price by using rebates to your advantage, without having to go through the hassle of negotiating back and forth with dealerships.
Rebates are always expiring, and you need to take advantage of available rebates before inventory runs out, or the manufacturer decides to cancel the rebate.
